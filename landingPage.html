Established in 1988

Discover a wide range of authentic Japanese foods, drinks, and snacks. We carry beloved brands such as Hawaiian Sun drinks, Aloha Shoyu, and favorites like Poi, Saimin, and Portuguese Sausage. Shop unique items like nori, edamame, miso paste, and natto. Craving something fresh? Enjoy our made-to-order sushi, poke bowls, and nigiri. We also offer party platters and catering to make your gatherings special.
